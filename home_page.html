ABOUT US
Xaivers College is an international instituition bases in Kenya. It mostly targets post highschool students who tend to study for their careers.
It was started in the year 2002, as per now it has more then thirty thousand students, from almost all parts of the world. The instituition was an idea brought up by Mr.Ebrah, who is a well known businessman.
Xaivers college intends to equip students with skills that will enebale them in their future in order to be more productie in their career path.
Just an overview, the college provides courses of different departments including:
  • Medical and Pharmacy department
  • Science department
  • ICT department
  • Engineering department
  • Business department
And many more.... you will find them under the academic section.

So far so good, the instituition has been running well and expanding more.We are the second largest college in Kenya, and still look up to be the best of all.
We have six awards so far, for the best instituitional perfomance.
The college is led by the abled principal, Mr. Jack Brown. He has been the principal for the las six years now and all we can see is more and more elevating.

ACADEMICS

Xaivers college intends to provide students with quality and latest education as per the syllabus. Both theory and practical lessons are well taught by our professional trainers.
The instituition also has a library, with many many books that students can read anytime. We use a certain criteria to enroll post high school students by checking on their previous perfomace in the national examination.


We provide different certification including:
  1. Advanced Diploma
  2. Degree
  3. Masters
  4. PhD

Semesters

In every year, there are three semesters, thats the period trainers learn. Each semester has 8-10 weeks.The trainees are expected to be attending all the classes without failure, unless otherwise. A whole semester will be used for the trainees' attachment/internship period, when the time comes.

Examinations

At the end of every semester, an internal exam is held to asses the stuents. At the middle of every semester, CATS will also be held. The end term exam may include practicals and theory too.
